About Us

Gauri Desai is a speech pathologist who has worked in a variety of settings. She has experience working in early intervention, pediatric outpatient clinic as well as hospitals. She has also worked in a University setting supervising graduate students to develop their clinical skills. She truly believes in treating the client as a whole thus connecting with family members and other professionals. Her passion is delivering high quality-effective treatment in- person (at the client’s home) as well as remotely because it’s easier for our clients to make progress toward meaningful communication goals when their sessions are convenient and easy to access. She is ASHA (American Speech and Hearing association) certified, NMES (Neuromuscular electrical stimulation for swallowing) certified, IM (Interactive metronome), Level 1 DIR (Developmental Individual-difference Relationship-based model) Floor time certified.

At Enrich Speech therapy, we will work with you to set up a treatment program that takes your individual needs into account thus delivering individualized care by incorporating Evidenced based Practice approaches.

Services

  • Individual therapy at home and tele therapy
  • Complete evaluations with individualized treatment plans
  • Consultations/collaboration with other professionals
  • Regular progress reports
  • Parent/caregiver meetings,updates on progress and carryover activities for the home setting

Speech therapy services for adults include:

  • Fluency Disorders
  • Articulation disorders
  • Aphasia and Stroke rehabilitation
  • Dysarthria
  • Dysphagia (swallowing disorders)
  • Executive functioning disorders

Speech therapy services for children:

  • Articulation and Phonological Disorders
  • Receptive and expressive language disorders
  • Stuttering and Fluency
  • Childhood Apraxia of Speech (CAS)
  • Voice Disorders
  • Pragmatics and social skills therapy

How Online Speech Therapy Works

Online Speech Therapy has been proven effective by over 40% academic studies. Dut to it's easy integration into even busiest schedules, it can facilitate even better results than in-person treatment.
